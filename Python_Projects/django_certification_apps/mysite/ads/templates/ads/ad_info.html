"Ads" is an application that allows logged-in users to view a list of ads along with the information of those ads.

It also allows logged-in users to create ads that other users can access and view.


  • Ads have a title and details, and they can also include an image and tags.
  • Ads can be searched by titles or tags using the search bar at the top right of the main ad page.
  • Ads can be favorited by clicking the star to the right of the ad on the main ad page.
  • Clicking the ad will bring you to its detail view, which contains all of the ads information along with a live-updating comment section for that ad.
  • Ownership is enforced in this application. Users can update and delete ads that they have made and cannot update nor delete the ads made by other users.
